Welcome to SwapShelf.san!

At SwapShelf, we bring together a vibrant community of book lovers eager to share, discover, and exchange books. Our platform is built on the belief that books are more than just pages—they are gateways to knowledge, imagination, and new perspectives. Whether you’re looking to give away books you’ve finished or discover new reads, SwapShelf makes it easy to connect with others who share your passion for literature.

How It Works:

  • List your Books
    Got books that you’ve already read and want to share? Simply list them on SwapShelf, and let other members discover your collection.
  • Explore New Reads
    Browse books shared by others in the community and discover new gems. Find your next great read through our user-friendly interface.
  • Easy Book Swaps
    Arrange a swap with fellow readers in just a few clicks. Connecting with other book lovers has never been this simple.

Our Mission:

At SwapShelf, we are committed to promoting sustainability through the recycling and reuse of books. Our mission is to foster meaningful connections among readers while minimizing waste. By encouraging book swaps, we aim to reduce the environmental impact of mass book production while enriching our community with shared literary experiences.

Why Choose SwapShelf?

  • Convenient
    Easily list and exchange books from the comfort of your home. Seamless doorstep delivery and pickup.
  • Affordable
    Exchange books with no extra cost beyond delivery. Save money and enjoy a rotating selection of new reads.
  • Community
    Build connections with fellow book lovers. Share your favourite reads and discover new ones form your community.

Safety First:

Your safety is our top priority.

  • Disinfected book handling and packaging
  • Safe, contactless delivery and collection
  • 48-hour quarantine before circulation

Quality is our priority:

Books are curated and delivered with care

With focus on providing high-quality books and ensuring a seamless experience with every exchange.

  • Handpicked Selection: Each book is reviewed to ensure it's in great condition
  • Personalised Recommendations: Get tailored suggestions based on your reading history.
  • Quick Delivery: Books are delivered promptly to keep your reading journey uninterrepted.
